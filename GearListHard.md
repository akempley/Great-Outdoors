
# Gear list for a hard hike
- Weather appropriate clothing, like shorts and Tshirt
- Change of clothes in case temp falls
- Backpack
- Hatchet
- Water proof lighter
- Sturdy, non-slip hiking boots
- Emergency Flares
- Water bottle
- Enough food to last incase of emergency, so MRE's might be a good idea.
- Compas
- First-Aide kit
- Bear mace
- Rain Jacket
- Hot-Hands
